    My research activities address system and software engineering in different application domains namely cyber-physical systems, Internet of things, embedded systems, and sustainability systems. In most of my current research I investigate:
    • Model-Driven (software) Engineering,
    • Domain Specific (Modeling) Languages,
    • Cloud Computing.
    The best way to have more details on my research is through my publications below.
